# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Local car owners and drivers in Myślenice and surrounding areas. They need a fast, reliable way to check workshop services, locate the shop, and order replacement parts (oil, filters, bulbs, batteries, tires) for pick up on their mobile or desktop devices.

## Product Purpose
To showcase MOTOBAJER's 16+ years of automotive service expertise, their official affiliation with the EuroWarsztat network, and their auto-parts shop. Success looks like increased local customer trust, higher call volumes, and simplified online parts reservation.

## Brand Personality
- Honest (*Uczciwy*)
- Expert (*Fachowy*)
- Modern (*Nowoczesny*)
Emotes professional network reliability while maintaining personal, high-quality local service trust.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, dated 2000s-era workshop websites.
- Overly-aggressive animation effects that slow down older phones.
- Flat, low-contrast grey-on-white text fields.
- Nested card grids or generic landing page templates.

## Design Principles
1. **Expert Confidence:** Visually reassure the user of quality through clean layouts, official EuroWarsztat branding, and authentic 5-star customer reviews.
2. **Immediate Utility:** Actionable information (phone number, address, open hours) must be visible instantly with clear `tel:` and directions triggers.
3. **Responsive Tactility:** Button targets, checkout steps, and cart items must feel fast, accessible, and highly optimized for touch screens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio >= 4.5:1 for body and muted copy.
- Full responsive reflow for mobile devices.
- Focus outlines and keyboard-navigable elements.
- Compliance with prefers-reduced-motion media query standards.
